About Us

Al-Ula is a land known for its historical, geological and geographical importance. This ancient province also derived its importance from its location at the crossroads of the Silk Road and the Incense Road, and now the world is rediscovering that land in which civilizations carved their traces.

From this rooted history, our philosophy in Najd Village is based on providing a convenient place rich in history and heritage with delicious traditional Najd meals prepared by experienced chefs under the direct supervision of specialists in authentic Najd cuisine.

OUR VISION

To offer the concept of rich and diverse Najd cuisine in its original style and in a beautiful heritage ambiance that nourishes feelings of gratitude and nostalgia.

OUR MISSION

To continue to have pride in our history while remembering the old Najd lifestyle and Arab cuisine in an environment that combines the uniqueness of the heritage and its architecture and antiques with the warmth of hospitality and generosity and quality of service, and above all .. the taste of excellence by relying on our long experience
